⚡ Quick install (run on your VPS as root)
bash <(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/IRNova/Tools/main/nova-backend.sh)

Paste this one line into your VPS over SSH. It installs Xray, writes the config, opens the firewall, and prints the exact Backend URL to paste above. Your VPS credentials are never sent anywhere, you run it yourself on your own server.

🛠 Change port / remove backend

Change the port, re-run the installer with a Cloudflare-allowed port (8080, 8880, 2052, 2082, 2086, 2095). Ports like 2076/3080/10000 do NOT work.

Remove the backend from your VPS, stops Xray and deletes it. (To stop using it in the panel, just turn the toggle off above; the client QR disappears.)

systemctl stop xray 2>/dev/null; systemctl disable xray 2>/dev/null; rm -rf /usr/local/etc/xray /usr/local/bin/xray /usr/local/share/xray /etc/systemd/system/xray.service /etc/systemd/system/xray@.service; systemctl daemon-reload; echo "Xray removed."

📘 How it works

Control all your Nova panels from this one. Add your other panels below, and this panel copies its settings, routing and clean IPs to them every time you save.

1
Add each panel
Press “+ Add a panel” and enter its name, its web address, and its admin password (the same one you log in with on that panel).
2
Save and sync
Press “Save & sync all”. Your settings are copied to every panel. It also syncs on its own each time you save here.
💡 Good to know
  • ⚠️ Syncing replaces the other panel’s settings with this one’s, including its ID, hosts and users. Only add panels you want to become copies of this hub.
  • Nothing to set up on the other panels. Just add them here with their password.
  • Each panel must be on its own Cloudflare account. A panel cannot control another one on the same account.
  • Keep every panel on the same Nova version (open /install/status on each to check).
  • If a panel’s admin password changes, update it here too.
